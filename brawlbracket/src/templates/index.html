Brawlhalla tournaments made simple.

Whether you're a player or a tournament operator, BrawlBracket is designed to make your tournament experience smooth and easy.

BrawlBracket's features make participating in a tournament as simple as possible, even for beginners.

Automatic matchmaking

No more searching for your opponents in group chats. You'll be automatically placed in a chat lobby with your opponent when your match is ready.

Built-in tourney rules

Automated legend picks and realm bans mean you won't have to memorize rules, making it easier than ever to jump into tournament play.

Desktop notifications

Optional notifications and sounds will let you know when your opponent is done their match, so you can do something else while you wait.

Steam integration

No need for another password — you already have an account. And you'll always know who the other players are because you can find them on Steam.

It's also designed from the ground up to make tournament management as painless as possible.

Everyone together

You don't have to hunt down and confirm missing players when registration, check-in, and match setup all take place in the same web app.

Comprehensive data

Know everything about your tournament's matches from who's holding them up, to how far they've progressed, to what room number they're playing in.

Customizable rulesets

Best of 17 grand finals, Thundergard and Gnash are banned, and winner picks the realm every time? You're weird, but we've got you covered.

Know who needs your help

Players have an easy button to contact you in times of need. They don't have to search for your contact info, and you don't have to watch them like a hawk.
